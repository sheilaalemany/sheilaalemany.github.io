About me

I am pursuing a dual degree with a B.S. in computer science and a B.S in mathematics. Upon graduation in May of 2019, I plan to pursue my Ph.D. in computer science to achieve my goal of conducting impactful research. My research interests include artificial intelligence, cybersecurity, and robotics. I'm interested in designing/enhancing robust, large-scale artificially intelligent systems that augment and extend the human knowledge/capabilities.

Throughout my undergraduate career, I have been fortunate enough to receive scholarships (i.e., Great Minds in STEM), outstanding research awards (CRA-E Finalist, FIU SCIS), and an internship at MIT Lincoln Laboratory. I have also thoroughly enjoyed teaching mathematics and computer sciences courses, and being a ballerina. I have learned valuable lessons about hard work and perseverance, and I am determined to use everything I have learned to make a positive impact in my graduate studies and in my life.
